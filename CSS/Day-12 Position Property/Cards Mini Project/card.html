Who were Alluri Sitarama Raju and Komaram Bheem from RRR? RRR is a completely fictional story constructed by SS Rajamouli and his father, Vijayendra Prasad. However, the movie is based on the lives of Telugu freedom fighters, namely Alluri Sitarama Raju and Komaran Bheem, played by Ram Charan and Jr NTR respectively.
